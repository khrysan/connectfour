Connect Four
---------------------------
To play:
- Launch:  https://khrysan.github.io/connectfour/
- Player 1 will click the dotted square above the column, this will drop a red circle piece on the lowest uninhabited cell.
- Player 2 will do the same and it will drop a blue circle piece.
- Repeat process back and forth until objective is achieved.
- Objective:  A win is awarded when there are 4 pieces of the same color in a line (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).
- When board is completely filled and there are no 4 pieces matching in a line, a tie is awarded.
- Select "Ok" in alert box (after a win or tie) to refresh the page and play again.
